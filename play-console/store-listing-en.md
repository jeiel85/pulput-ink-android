## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Pulpit Ink is a mobile sermon assistant for pastors, seminarians, and anyone who wants to turn spoken messages into well-organized written documents. Tap once to record, and OpenAI Whisper plus Google Gemini will transcribe the audio, clean up scripture references, and compile a structured sermon outline — all in a single, focused workflow.

KEY FEATURES

■ One-tap sermon recording
High-quality audio capture from the pulpit, the office, or the car. Live status indicators confirm the microphone is active throughout the session.

■ AI transcription with OpenAI Whisper
Whisper accurately turns spoken sermons (Korean and English) into text. Use either the cloud API mode for top accuracy, or download an on-device Whisper model (tiny / base / small) to transcribe fully offline.

■ Scripture references, formatted by Gemini
Gemini automatically detects scripture citations in your message and rewrites them in standard form — "Genesis 1:1", "John 3:16" — including bold formatting, normalized book names (Korean and English), and spacing fixes.

■ AI-generated sermon outline
From your edited transcript, Gemini produces a markdown sermon outline — introduction, body, conclusion, key message, application points. Re-compile anytime as the transcript evolves.

■ Library and search
Every sermon is stored in a local on-device database, searchable by title, body text, or tag. Your sermons are not permanently stored on any external server.

■ Edit, copy, share
Edit transcripts block by block. Copy the transcript, the outline, or the full sermon package to the clipboard, or share to any other app (Notes, Notion, messengers, email).

WHO IT'S FOR

- Pastors and ministers preparing weekly sermons
- Lay preachers who want a written record of their messages
- Seminary students transcribing lectures and Bible studies
- Anyone who wants to turn voice memos into structured documents

PRIVACY & SECURITY

By default, recorded audio and transcribed text stay on your device. When you invoke AI transcription or scripture formatting, the relevant data is sent over HTTPS to OpenAI and Google Gemini APIs strictly for processing. See the privacy policy for full details.

API KEY NOTE

This app uses the OpenAI Whisper API and the Google Gemini API. You will need to provide your own API keys to use the cloud transcription and Gemini formatting features. The on-device Whisper models work without any API key.
```
