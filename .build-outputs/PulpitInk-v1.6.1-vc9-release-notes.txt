Hotfix: transcript data loss fix (v1.6.1)

- [Critical fix] Resolved an issue where the transcript detail screen appeared empty even though transcription had completed. A foreign key cascade in our local database was wiping the just-written transcript segments immediately after save. All new transcripts now persist correctly.
- [Silent/very-short recordings] Recordings with no speech or that end within a second no longer fail. They now finish cleanly with a "No speech recognized" placeholder instead of an error badge.
- Note: sermons created in earlier versions whose transcript appears blank cannot be recovered — delete them and re-record. All transcripts going forward are saved properly.
